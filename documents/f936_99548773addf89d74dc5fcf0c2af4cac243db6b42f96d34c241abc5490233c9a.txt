        Registration Opens February 2022
        &nbsp;
        As the world's biggest library event, the onsite Annual Conference &amp; Exhibition&nbsp;brings together thousands of&nbsp;librarians and library staff, educators, authors, publishers, friends of libraries, trustees, special guests, and exhibitors. You'll find educational programming; important announcements and updates;&nbsp;relevant legislation and policies;&nbsp;and discussions&nbsp;that majorly impact&nbsp;libraries, their roles,&nbsp;and their ongoing transformation. &nbsp;
        Event Highlights include:
        
         100s of top&nbsp;authors&nbsp;and unforgettable&nbsp;speakers
         900+ expert&nbsp;exhibitors, new products, services, and titles
         Quality&nbsp;and&nbsp;scope&nbsp;of educational programs, topics, and formats
         News You Can Use sessions with updates on policies, technology, and more
         Fun and entertaining&nbsp;networking activities and social&nbsp;events
