      Noun 
      information science (countable and uncountable, plural information sciences)  
      
       (applied science) A broad and interdisciplinary science that deals with the gathering, classification, storage, manipulation, retrieval and analysis of information as an organized resource.
       
      Synonyms 
      
       information studies
